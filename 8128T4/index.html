Language Center

Together We Complete Each Other

Cultural communication among nations shall not be performed only by learning the languages. The mission of transmitting and teaching languages is the core of the human communication process. Moreover, spreading peace and tolerance as well as introducing the other nations’ cultures is a human duty.
The perspective of establishing the Eloquence and Development Institute in Nizwa has emerged taking into consideration the cultural, historical and geographical dimensions of this Willaya. The Institute focuses on teaching Arabic as a second language as well as courses in the other international languages such as the English, French, Chinese and Japanese languages, and organising development courses in the administrative, planning, technological and human resources fields of knowledge.
The Vision: Innovation and excellence in providing practical training and learning that contributes to teaching languages and developmental programs.
The Mission: Quality of teaching, adequacy of work, cooperation and development at stimulus environment.
The Objectives: apply the best teaching and training practices by using unique curriculums to teach the languages, and teaching Arabic for non-Arabic speakers, and provide programs that fulfil the local and regional societies.
  1. The Language Center is an on-campus resource for both students and faculty to enhance and encourage the foreign language learning process. Our goal is to create an environment through the use of technology and face to face communication that helps students achieve their language learning goals.  
  2. For students, we offer an array of media (print, television, and film) in a variety of languages, so that students are able to experience the language in an everyday setting. For those who wish to hold group study sessions, we offer small rooms. These rooms may be used by students currently enrolled in a foreign language class with a lab fee.
  3. Instructors are able to reserve conversation rooms and media using our online reservation system. In addition, instructors may also request to have recordings made from our selection of international satellite channels. For details, please see the "Services For Instructors" link below.
